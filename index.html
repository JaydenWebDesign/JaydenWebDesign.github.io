Slick

2025

The Poet: Jayden Gladden

Slick...
Ain't...
It...

Slick went to the store

Grabbed himself a coffee

And immediately dropped it on the floor

Slick...
Ain't...
It...

Slick decided to go home

Watch some tv on the couch

As soon as he goes to sit down, the couch breaks on impact

Slick...
Ain't...
It...

The End.
